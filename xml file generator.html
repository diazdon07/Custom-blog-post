]]> Wynner parents Nicole Mims Johnson and Riian Wynn received the Marcia Wynn Award at our Fall Kickoff held on Sept. 25, 2025. The Marcia Wynn Award honors parents in recognition of their extraordinary service to the JWF community.

Nicole Mims Johnson has served on the board for over 3 years, and serves on the Red Tie Soirée and Awards Ceremony planning committees. As a member of the Family Engagement Committee, Nicole is welcoming towards and strives to engage all Wynners and their family members. Nicole’s thoughtfulness, insight, and creative problem solving greatly benefit the JWF.

As a Wynner parent, board member of 20 years(!) and member of the Wynn family, Riian Wynn has given so much to the JWLA. She currently serves as the vice president and chair of the Board Development Committee. She contributes to every major JWF event. Her positivity, dedication, and leadership help the JWF continually improve and evolve.

Wynners Amaya Johnson and Kamerin Stephens presented their mothers with the 2025 Marcia Wynn Award on behalf of the Justin Wynn Board of Directors. Congratulations, Nicole and Riian!

]]> Wynner parents Todd Cherkasky and Joe McQuade received the Marcia Wynn Award at our Family/Board Fall Kickoff held on Sept. 26, 2024. The Marcia Wynn Award honors parents in recognition of their extraordinary service to the JWF community.

Todd has served on the board for 5 years, and has helped out with the Red Tie and Awards Ceremony, and now is serving on the Family Engagement Committee. He has many times spoken to the parents of new Wynners about his appreciation for and dedication to the Justin Wynn Fund.

Joe has served on the board for 5 years, has helped out with the 3-on-3 every year, and has served on the Marketing Committee and now the Resource Development Committee. His thoughtful and helpful approach are most appreciated.

Wynners Micah Cherkasky and Anna McQuade presented their fathers with the 2024 Marcia Wynn Award on behalf of the Justin Wynn Board of Directors. Congratulations Todd and Joe!
